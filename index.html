Pat Bing Soo can have:

  • red beans
  • rice cakes
  • condensed milk
  • fruit

There are different kinds of Bingsu of course. "Pat" bingsu means red beans+shaved ice. Where as fruit bingsu means just fruit+shaved ice (and ice cream, condensed milk etc.)
