Pat-Bing-Su

Say it with me. Pat Bing Su. It is sooooo good and one of my go to summer treats. It's shaved ice + a variety of toppings. Here's is a winter and summer bingsu. You can hover over the images to see it more clearly.





Pat Bing Soo can have:
  • red beans
  • rice cakes
  • condensed milk
  • fruit

There are different kinds of Bingsu of course. "Pat" bingsu means red beans+shaved ice. Where as fruit bingsu means just fruit+shaved ice (and ice cream, condensed milk etc.)
